About

Academic work with a public-facing mission

My research examines how policy design shapes educational outcomes, health, labor market experiences, and access to opportunity. Across these areas, I am especially interested in whether program rules, timing, incentives, and institutional design produce meaningful differences in everyday lives.

SC EPIC

South Carolina Economic Policy Center

SC EPIC connects decision-makers in and beyond South Carolina to applied economic research from the Economics Department at the Darla Moore School of Business. The center supports policy analysis, program evaluation, applied research, and practical engagement with public and private partners.
